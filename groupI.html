Abraham Lincoln was born on February 12th, 1809 and died on April 15th 1865. He was born in Hodgenville, Kentucky, he grew up on the west frontier in Kentucky and Indiana. He was an American politician and lawyer who served as the 16th President of the United States from March 1861 until his assassination in April 14th, 1865. Abraham Lincoln was assassinated by John Wilkes Booth on Good Friday, April 14, 1865.

Canada is the northern part of North America. It has 10 provinces and 3 territories. Canada is the second-largest country by area and the fourth-largest by land area. The 1867 Constitution Act officially declared Canadian Confederation on July 1, 1867, initially with four provinces: Ontario, Quebec, Nova Scotia, and New Brunswick.
